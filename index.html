Fr. Agnel Higher Secondary School

H I S T O R Y 

Fr. Agnel Higher Secondary School was started initially with Arts and Commerce Streams by late Fr. Philomeno D'Costa, S.F.X in June 1979
to meet demands of the time and the need of youth from the neighbourhood.

In the subsequent year, June 1982, the Science Stream was also started.
Later, vocationalization of education vocational training was introduced under theGoa Central Govt Scheme.
The Vocational Stream began with four courses: C.T, AET, OM and BI.

.

Fr. Agnel Higher Secondary is run under the aegis of the Xaverian Educational Society,
a duly registered society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

Ven.Fr.Agnel D'souza

A humble, peace-loving and inspiring member of the Society of Pilar, born on 21st Jan 1869 in the village of Anjuna, Goa.
It was the feast of St. Agnes—hence he was named Agnel, meaning “little lamb.”

He was the sixth child of nine (8 boys & 1 girl).
His father, Miguel Arcanjo Mariano de Souza, died when Agnel was 11; his mother, Maria Sinforsa Perpetua Magalhaes,died when he was 13.
